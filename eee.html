Department of Electrical Engineering

Established in 1921, the Department of Electrical Engineering is one of the oldest departments in the country. It offers B.Tech. programs in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, along with M.Tech. programs in Power Systems, Power Electronics, and Control & Instrumentation. The department has a highly qualified faculty actively engaged in research, with numerous publications in reputed journals and conferences.

The department has contributed significantly by introducing programs in Electronics & Communication Engineering in 1968 and Computer Science & Engineering in 1982. In 1994, the Department of Electrical Engineering was bifurcated to form separate departments. The department provides state-of-the-art infrastructure, including Electrical Machines Labs, Power Electronics Labs, Microprocessor Labs, and Instrumentation Labs, equipped with industry-standard software like MiPower, PSpice, and MATLAB.
