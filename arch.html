Department of Architecture

Established in 1967, the Department of Architecture at UVCE was the first in Karnataka to offer a Bachelor's degree in Architecture under the leadership of Prof. S.G. Pardhy. Located at the Jnana Bharathi Campus, the department boasts a well-equipped library serving as a valuable resource for students and faculty alike.

The department has a consultancy wing, "The Design Cell," which undertakes various projects for the university. Students have consistently excelled academically, winning numerous awards at state and national competitions organized by NASA. Alumni from the department have distinguished themselves in their respective fields both in India and abroad, making significant contributions to the profession.
