Department Of Civil

The Department of Civil Engineering was started in 1917 offering a B.E. course in Civil Engineering and has been accredited by AICTE New Delhi. The department was initially located in the city campus of Bangalore University at K R Circle until 1974 and later shifted to Jnana Bharathi Campus.

The department offers undergraduate (B.E.) and postgraduate (M.E.) programs in eight different fields of specialization. It is a recognized Quality Improvement Programme Department under MHRD, admitting M.E. and PhD candidates. The department focuses on producing skilled civil engineers and researchers while maintaining strong R&D capabilities. The UG curriculum balances engineering fundamentals and applications, integrating CAD, technical visits, project tours, and design projects.
